About me

Hi. I'm John Vaughan, a digital creative based in Newcastle, Australia.
I currently work as Creative Director and Lead Developer for a boutique full service brand agency and occasionally take on freelance projects.

I've always liked creating things, whether it's visual, code or musical. Something that I can be proud of and ideally helps people. I'm lucky enough to have created a career pursuing this passion.

In any given day I can jump from brainstorming brand strategy to sketching a new character concept, through coding a module or writing unit tests. That's one varied day.

With a degree in Art Theory and Digital Media, I've had 5 years working as a graphic designer in corporate design studios, 6 years working as a hands on Creative Director for a full service agency and 12 years concurrent digital freelance experience working across a range of graphic and digital projects. On a daily basis I use Adobe suite, HTML, CSS, SCSS, Javascript, Wordpress, PHP, SQL. I'm also familiar with several other CMS's, Node, Python, Typescript, @angular, RethinkDB, MongoDB, LESS, Webpack. I'm a big fan of lodash and had limited experience with Pug and Backbone. I'm learning React. I could dust off my Actionscript and even Lingo skills if needed.

I like guitars. I like painting. I like beautiful code. I love Manchester United. When I'm not working as Creative Director for Thurnham Teece or playing guitar in the Frenchman Street Jazz Band, I'm developing Layla - a music app that helps you listen to better music and support the musicians of tomorrow. It's written in @angular with a node server and a RethinkDB connection pushing realtime streams to clients via socketio.
